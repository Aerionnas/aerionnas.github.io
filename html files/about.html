About Me

Hi! My name is Aerionna Stephenson. I live in Atlanta, Georgia. I am a recent alumna of Brandeis University with a dual degree in Hispanic Studies and Latin American, Caribbean and Latinx Studies. During my undergraduate experience, I produced several projects centering on Afro-Latinx and Indigenous peoples, and I continue to make efforts to connect people across the diasporas. In addition, I was afforded the opportunity to take several Computer Science courses covering a wide range of topics such as Core Java, Python, Racket, Three.js, the Lean Startup process, and Discrete Structures. Of these topics, I feel the most passionate about Java. I have a solid foundation in the fundamentals, basic algorithms, Object Oriented Programming, file handling using CRUD operations, and Graphical User Interfaces. I am always looking for new ways to cultivate these skills and expand my expertise.
Since graduating from college, I participated in the Opportunity College Career Jumpstart Fellowship Program. I took a course in Junior Web Development, and I was able to master the fundamentals of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6, Twitter Bootstrap, jQuery, and Mocha. In this course, I demonstrated my ability to make contributions in a team setting, meet deadlines for assignments, work independently to learn new topics, display a strong attention to detail, critically think to find solutions to problems, and ask questions when necessary. I was able to create this portfolio website and a Task planner web application. Upon completing the program, I successfully earned my W3Schools JavaScript Certification. I am currently taking the Google UX Design Professional Certificate Coursera Course to improve the user experience of my web applications.
I describe myself as being multi-dimensional and able to adapt to any environment that I am placed. In addition to my technical knowledge, I am strong in the following areas: leadership, growth mindset, persistence, attention to detail, and communication. These skills afford me the ability to operate as a well-rounded individual able to take on any challenge. In addition, I have a plethora of experiences that I am able draw from in moments of uncertainty. With this skills and my technical background, I am confident that I will thrive in a professional setting. I am very excited to put these skills into practice and to contribute to a team setting. My passion is to create user-friendly websites for businesses and use my technical experience to find solutions to global issues.
