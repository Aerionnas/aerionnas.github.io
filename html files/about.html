About Me

Hi! My name is Aerionna. I live in Atlanta, Georgia. I am a recent alumnus of Brandeis University where I obtained a double major in Hispanic Studies and Latin American, Caribbean and Latinx Studies. During my undergraduate experience, I was able to take several courses in the area of Computer Science, where I predominantly focused on Back-End Development.
In June 2022, I participated in the Opportunity College Career Jumpstart Fellowship Program to supplement my studies in Technology. In the Junior Web Development Course, that was offered through the program, I was able to cultivate my skills in Front-End Web Development.
Now, I am pursuing a full time position as a Full Stack Developer. I am very excited to put these skills into practice and to contribute to a team setting. My passion is to create user-friendly websites for businesses and use my technical experience to find solutions to global issues.
I describe myself as being someone who is multi-dimensional and able to adapt to any environment. In addition to my technical knowledge, I am strong in the following behavioral skills: leadership, growth mindset, persistence, attention to detail, and interpersonal skills. These skills afford me the ability to operate as a well-rounded individual able to take on any challenge. In addition, I have a plethora of experiences that I am able draw from in moments of uncertainty.
